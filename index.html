Welcome!

The primary goal of this application is to provide a tool for you to store your lottery numbers and have them automatically checked to see if you are a winner. To begin you'll need to enter your lottery numbers and the date of the drawing. Make sure to hit save! If your numbers are drawn then your saved ticket will light up.
